priority: number

The priority for displaying such place. The larger the number the higher the priority. When queried, places are returned by decreasing priority. at small zoom levels, only the places with the highest priority will be displayed. For example, this could mean that only the museum is shown at zoom level 10 and that the pieces of art are only shown at zoom level 18
